Our Featured Trips

Velocity Drop

Experience the thrill of Class III-IV rapids on this action-packed journey down the river. Perfect for adrenaline seekers, this trip includes professional guides, safety gear, and a riverside lunch.

Flow Rider

Drift along calm, scenic waters on the Flow Rider — a relaxing rafting experience perfect for all ages. Surrounded by nature’s beauty, you’ll enjoy gentle currents, spot local wildlife, and create unforgettable memories on this guided float trip.

Rapid Rush

Perfect for beginners and intermediate paddlers, Rapid Rush offers a smooth blend of excitement and relaxation. Paddle through mild rapids as the sun begins to set, taking in scenic views and enjoying a peaceful ride capped off with a light riverside snack.
